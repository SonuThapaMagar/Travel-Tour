About Us

Welcome to Yarsa Tour and Travel!

At Yarsa Tour and Travel, we believe in creating unforgettable travel experiences that enrich your life. Based in the heart of Nepal, we are a dedicated team of travel enthusiasts committed to providing you with the highest quality travel services. Whether you are seeking adventure, culture, or relaxation, we have the perfect journey waiting for you.


Founded in 2020, Yarsa Tour and Travel started with a vision to share the beauty and diversity of Nepal with the world. Our founders, passionate travelers themselves, wanted to create a travel company that prioritizes customer satisfaction, sustainability, and authentic experiences. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the travel industry, known for our personalized service and attention to detail.


Top Destinations

Discover the most beautiful places in the world to visit.

Pokhara, Nepal

Known for its stunning natural beauty, Pokhara offers breathtaking views of the Annapurna range, serene lakes like Phewa Lake, and adventurous activities such as paragliding, boating, and trekking.

Rara, Nepal

Nestled in the remote Himalayas, Rara Lake is the largest lake in Nepal and offers pristine natural beauty. The area is ideal for hiking, boating, and enjoying the tranquility of nature.

Chitwan, Nepal

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Chitwan National Park is renowned for its wildlife, including Bengal tigers, one-horned rhinoceroses, and elephants

Manang, Nepal

Located in the Annapurna region, Manang is a picturesque valley surrounded by majestic Himalayan peaks.

Mustang, Nepal

Mustang, also known as the "Forbidden Kingdom," is a remote and mystical region in north-central Nepal, bordering Tibet.

Tour Overview

Nestled in the Langtang National Park, Gosaikunda is a sacred alpine lake that attracts trekkers and pilgrims alike. Surrounded by towering peaks and beautiful alpine landscapes, it is a serene and spiritual destination. The trek to Gosaikunda is challenging, offering breathtaking views of the Himalayas and a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu

It is a panoramic thrill flying into Kathmandu on a clear day. You will be greeted by stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayan peaks, beginning a memorable journey. After checking into your hotel, you will be briefed about your upcoming activities. The rest of the day is at leisure for you to explore or relax.

Day 2: Sightseeing in Kathmandu

Today, explore the cultural heritage of Kathmandu. Visit the Swayambhunath Stupa (Monkey Temple), Pashupatinath Temple, and Boudhanath Stupa. In the evening, enjoy a local cultural performance and a traditional Nepali dinner.

Day 3: Travel to Pokhara

After breakfast, travel to Pokhara by road or flight. Enjoy the stunning views of the mountains during the journey. Upon arrival in Pokhara, visit the serene Phewa Lake for a boat ride and relax by the lakeside.

Day 4: Explore Pokhara

Explore the beautiful city of Pokhara, including the World Peace Pagoda, Deviâ€™s Fall, and the Gupteshwor Cave. Enjoy a beautiful sunset view from Sarangkot, offering panoramic views of the Annapurna and Machapuchare mountain ranges.

Day 5: Travel to Gosaikunda

After breakfast, take a scenic drive and trek towards Gosaikunda. Gosaikunda is a high-altitude lake and a sacred site for Hindus. You will enjoy stunning views of the Langtang range and alpine forests as you ascend. Camp by the lake and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Day 6: Explore Gosaikunda

Explore the Gosaikunda lake and its surrounding area. You can visit other smaller lakes nearby or hike to a viewpoint for panoramic views of the Langtang, Ganesh Himal, and other surrounding peaks. This is a peaceful and spiritual place, perfect for reflection and nature walks.

Day 7: Return to Kathmandu

After breakfast, begin your descent back to Kathmandu. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax or do some last-minute shopping. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner with your tour group.
